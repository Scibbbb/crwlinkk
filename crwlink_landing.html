FAQ

Common questions

What exactly is Crwlink?
▾
Crwlink is an all-in-one platform built specifically for IRL streamers, live creators, and their crew. It replaces a bunch of separate tools — rate calculators, clip editors, media kit builders, gig boards — with one platform built around the actual IRL streaming workflow.
Is it only for IRL streamers?
▾
Nope — it works for any live streamer on Twitch, Kick, YouTube, or TikTok, as well as camera operators, clip editors, social media managers, and tech runners who work with streamers. Brands looking to sponsor creators also use the brand deals board.
How does the rate calculator work?
▾
You enter your real stats — average concurrent viewers, platform followers, TikTok audience, chat activity, niche, and location — and Crwlink calculates your rates based on real industry CPM data. It gives you a full rate card with overlay ads, dedicated segments, social post rates, and package deals, plus a monthly estimated brand revenue figure.
What's the difference between the free plan and paid plans?
▾
The free plan gives you the rate calculator, the ability to browse gigs, 3 brand deal views per month, and 4 free courses in the Academy. Paid plans unlock the ability to apply to gigs, create a crew profile, access AI tools, export your media kit, run analytics, use the clip studio, and apply to unlimited brand deals.
When does Crwlink fully launch?
▾
We're currently in beta with over 800 early users. The full public launch is coming soon. Join the waitlist and you'll get first access plus a free 30-day Pro trial when we go live.
Is this only for big streamers?
▾
Not at all. The rate calculator works from 0 viewers to 500K+. The Academy is specifically built for new creators starting from scratch. And the gig board has work for camera ops and editors at every level — from beginner setups to full IRL productions.
Can I cancel my plan anytime?
▾
Yes — no contracts, no commitments, no cancellation fees. Cancel anytime from your account settings and you keep access until the end of your billing period.
